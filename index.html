Features

Leave Your Mark

Post media to a specific location and whenever a user passes that specific location they will be able to see your media. This app is unique because everyone you decide to can see the media you've posted at a location forever!

Want everyone to know that you were the first person to accomplish something at that one place. Well tag it and let the world know!

Advertise

Allow everyone within feet or miles to see media that you have posted. Great way to advertise to many people all at one time.

Want a ton of people to hear your newest song, well post it at a high volume location.

Interact With Friends

Connect with friends through the app and share your awesome media. Leave little teasers for your friends so that they have to visit the location in order to view the media. Great way to communicate, and have fun doing it.

Want your girlfriend or boyfriend to read a poem at the place where you guys met? Well create a poem at that location and send it off to them.
